Free NECTA-aligned resources for Tanzanian secondary school students.

Built for Students

Science Study Hub is a free learning platform designed specifically for Tanzanian secondary school students preparing for NECTA examinations. Our mission is to make quality science education accessible to every student, wherever they are.

All materials are aligned with the Tanzania Institute of Education (TIE) curriculum and regularly reviewed to reflect current examination patterns.
